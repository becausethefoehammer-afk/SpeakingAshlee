Privacy Policy

Speaking Ashlee — Last updated: June 23, 2026

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Speaking Ashlee does not require an account, does not track usage, and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Text You Enter

Text you type into the app is sent to our server solely to generate a translation. It is not logged, stored, or used for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

Translations are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Text you enter is processed by Anthropic in accordance with Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
